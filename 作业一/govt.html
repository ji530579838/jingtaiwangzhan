Avoid queues at our Service Centre

We are running a reduced service at 101 Wakefield Street due to the impact of the 14 November earthquake on our service centre.
Avoid queues
- For Council invoices such as rates and parking coupons, use Pay online.
- For parking fines and parking permits, continue to use our usual online payment systems.
- Get new rubbish bags, recycling bags and glass crates from Johnsonville, Kilbirnie, Newtown and Tawa libraries.
- Get help dealing with the aftermath of the 14 November earthquake at our one-stop shop, ERIC
- See a problem in the city? Use our online Fix It form.
